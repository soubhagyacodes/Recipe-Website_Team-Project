Dumplings with chicken filling 

				Ingredients:

				1 cup chicken mince
				100 grams dim sum dough
				1-2 green chillies, finely chopped
				2 tablespoons sliced spring onion greens
				1½ teaspoons coriander powder
				¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
				1 teaspoon red chilli powder
				½ teaspoon cumin powder
				½ teaspoon garam masala powder
				Salt to taste
				1 tablespoon chopped fresh coriander leaves
				Refined flour for dusting
				Schezwan sauce for serving

				Method:

				1. Heat some water in a deep non-stick pan. Place a steamer, cover and let the water boil.
				2. Mix together chicken mince, green chillies, spring onion greens, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chilli powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, salt and coriander leaves in a bowl.
				3. Divide the dough into large equal portions. Dust the worktop with some flour and roll out each portion into thin sheets. 
				4. Cut each sheet into roundels using a medium size cookie cutter. 
				5. Put some chicken mixture in the center of each roundel, apply some water on the edges fold, pinch one edge, press it in and repeat till the end to seal.
				6. Place the dim sums in the hot steamer, cover and steam for 8-10 minutes.
				7. Serve hot with schezwan sauce.
