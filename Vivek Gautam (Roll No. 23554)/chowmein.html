Veg-Chow-Mein

				Ingredients:

					2 cups boiled noodles
					2-3 spring onion bulbs, sliced
					5-6 button mushrooms, cut into quarters
					3-4 babycorn, diagonally sliced and blanched
					¼ cup mixed capsicum diamonds (red, green and yellow)
					1 medium onion, sliced
					1 tablespoon sesame oil 
					4-5 garlic cloves, sliced
					Salt to taste
					1 teaspoon soy sauce
					1 tablespoon red chilli sauce
					1 teaspoon vinegar
					1 teaspoon toasted white sesame seeds + for garnishing
					Diagonally sliced spring onion greens for garnishing

				Method:

					1. Heat sesame oil in a non-stick pan. Add garlic, ginger and spring onion bulbs and sauté for 1 minute. 
					2. Add mushrooms and babycorn and sauté for 1 minute. Add salt and mix. Add mixed capsicums diamonds and onion and sauté for 1 minute. 
					3. Add noodles, soy sauce, chilli sauce and salt, mix and cook for 2 minutes. Add vinegar and 1 teaspoon sesame seeds and mix well. 
					4. Serve hot garnished with spring onion greens and sesame seeds
