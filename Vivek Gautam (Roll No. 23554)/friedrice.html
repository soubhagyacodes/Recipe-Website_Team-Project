Chicken-Fried-Rice

				Ingredients:

					Omelette
					4 eggs
					Salt to taste
					Black pepper powder to taste
					4 tsps sesame oil
					2 tsps chopped garlic
					1 tsp chopped ginger
					200 grams chicken leg boneless, cut into ½ cm pieces
					1½ cups chicken stock
					½ tsp dark soy sauce
					2 tbsps chopped spring onion greens
					Rice
					1 tsp + 1 tbsp sesame oil
					1 egg
					Salt to taste
					Black pepper powder to taste
					1 tbsp chopped garlic
					½ tbsp chopped ginger
					2-3 tbsps chopped spring onion bulbs
					2-3 tbsps chopped red capsicum
					3 cups cooked rice
					2-3 tbsps chopped spring onion greens
					1 tsp vinegar
					Shrdded nori sheets for garnish

				Method:

					1. For omelette, break eggs in a bowl. Add salt, black pepper powder and whisk well.
					2. Heat sesame oil in a small nonstick pan. Add garlic, ginger and sauté for 30 seconds.
					3. Add chicken and sauté on high heat for 3-4 minutes. Add chicken stock and once the mixture comes to a boil, add dark soy sauce, salt, black pepper powder and mix well. Cook on medium heat for 5-6 minutes.
					4. Add the egg mixture, cover and cook on medium heat for 3-4 minutes. Sprinkle spring onion greens on top.
					5. For rice, heat 1 tsp sesame oil in a nonstick wok. Break egg, add salt, black pepper powder and scramble for 30 seconds. Transfer in a plate and set aside.
					6. Heat remaining sesame oil in the same wok, add garlic, ginger, spring onion bulbs and sauté for a minute.
					7. Add red capsicum and sauté for 30 seconds.
					8. Add cooked rice, black pepper powder, salt and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes or till the rice is heated thoroughly.
					9. Add scrambled eggs, spring onion greens, vinegar and mix well.
					10. Transfer the prepared rice in a serving bowl, place the prepared omelette on top, garnish with nori sheets and serve hot.
